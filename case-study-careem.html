Careem: Transforming Transportation

How BrickLinks helped Careem expand its market presence and enhance brand loyalty through strategic marketing initiatives

180% User Growth
2.5M App Downloads
68% Brand Recall
40% Cost Reduction

The Challenge & Our Solution

Understanding the problem and implementing effective strategies

The Challenge

Careem faced intense competition in the ride-hailing market and needed to differentiate itself while expanding across diverse Middle Eastern markets with varying cultural norms, languages, and consumer behaviors.

  • Low brand recognition compared to global competitors
  • Cultural barriers in different regional markets
  • High customer acquisition costs
  • Need for localized marketing approaches
  • Driver recruitment and retention challenges

Our Solution

BrickLinks developed a comprehensive, hyper-localized marketing strategy that respected cultural nuances while maintaining brand consistency across all markets.

  • Developed region-specific campaigns for each market
  • Created a multi-tier referral program to drive organic growth
  • Implemented data-driven optimization of marketing spend
  • Designed culturally relevant content for all channels
  • Built strategic partnerships with local businesses and events

Our Approach

  • Developed hyper-localized marketing campaigns for each region with cultural sensitivity
  • Created a referral program that increased user acquisition by 65% while reducing costs
  • Implemented data-driven optimization of marketing spend across all channels
  • Designed culturally relevant content for social media channels in multiple languages
  • Built strategic partnerships with local businesses and events to increase brand visibility
  • Optimized the user onboarding experience for higher conversion and retention rates
  • Developed targeted driver recruitment campaigns to expand service coverage
